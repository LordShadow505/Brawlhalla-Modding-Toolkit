# SVG Maker

The SVG Maker facilitates the creation and post-processing of SVG files, optimizing paths and nodes to ensure compatibility and high performance when injected back into the game engine.

## Overview

Brawlhalla's engine has strict requirements for how SVG files are formatted and structured. The SVG Maker ensures that your custom artwork is automatically optimized, preventing crashes and rendering errors in-game.
