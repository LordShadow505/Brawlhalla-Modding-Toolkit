# Fast Shape Replacer

The Fast Shape Replacer streamlines the process of injecting new vector shapes into the game's SWF files. It automates the replacement process, saving significant time when updating complex character assets.

## Overview

Manually replacing shapes in FFDEC can be a tedious process, especially when working on a large mod with multiple assets. The Fast Shape Replacer automates this workflow, allowing you to quickly swap out multiple shapes at once.
